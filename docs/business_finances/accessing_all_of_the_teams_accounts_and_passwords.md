# Accessing The Team's Accounts and Passwords

The team keeps all of their passwords through a Bitwarden account whose username and password is pinned in the officer channel in the Autoboat discord. If you can't find the Bitwarden account for whatever reason, then please message/ email one of the officers/ past officers and they should be able to provide it to you. Specifically you can email me at [chrisjnassif@gmail.com](mailto:chrisjnassif@gmail.com) and I can give you the login information for the Bitwarden account.

We have everything from our team AWS, Docker Hub, Github, Cloudflare, Tailscale, Instagram, etc accounts in here. It makes it very nice to not have to remember so many different accounts and have everything in a single place. One of the main things that is not in here is the [autoboat@vt.edu](mailto:autoboat@vt.edu) email address. Please see [Getting Access to the AutoBoat Email Documentation](getting_access_autoboat_team_email.md) to learn how to access that. The other main thing that's not here is the login to the Roboflow, but for Roboflow, we simply have to do "continue with email" and enter the [autoboat@vt.edu](mailto:autoboat@vt.edu) email and then it will send an email to the [autoboat@vt.edu](mailto:autoboat@vt.edu) email address to verify that you should really be able to login to the Roboflow. Once you press the button on the email they send you, you should be logged in. It's that simple!
