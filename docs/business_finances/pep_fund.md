# PEP Funding 

This is probably the most simple of all of the funding sources, where the head organizer of the PEP competition will simply give you a $5000 check to whatever account you want, and you can do whatever you want with that money. There are no limits, no application, no oversight on what you can spend the money on, no reimbursement system, no deadlines to spend, etc etc. You just transfer the money to a bank account and then you can do whatever you want with that.
